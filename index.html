About Me

An Engineer who is passionate about Data Science.
Loves Mathematics and worked on Deep Learning, AI and Blockchain Technology.

In short I'm a 'Numbers' guy and a problem 'Solver'.

Cochin, a city in southwest India's coastal Kerala state, is my hometown. During my childhood, I moved to Kingdom Of Saudi Arabia where I brought up till my middle school. I was fortunate enough to grow up with the rapid changes in technologies. I got to witness some of the cutting edge technologies and the advent of other technologies as well. The first computer language I learned was HTML - back when during my high school I came to know about what programming is after attending a class on HTML and during my time HTML was a thing that you could customize and create funny webpages. This happened at the age of 12. After buffing up a bit on HTML in high school, my first year of college unfolded that made me introduce to Java and Python which was next few computer languages I learned henceforth. Ever since then, I've considered myself devoted to computer science. After digging and grinding up a little bit, I came to know that I am more interested on mathematical stuffs and during the end of my college days I found out Data Science is the 'One' followed by Deep Learning, AI and Blockchain Technology.


That's how I ❤ Data Science and took up my career as a Data Scientist.

I am a novice writer who loves reading fantasy fictions and mysteries. Also a great fan of Sherlock Holmes and the mysteries of Agatha Christie. Hobbies are swimming, playing basketball, cricket, chess and a curious reader of space sciences such as astronomy and astrophysics.
